Avoid Complex Words

Use shorter words that aren't ambiguous

Shorter words are often more accurate and less ambiguous. Writers sometimes use long, complex words as a way to distance themselves from the text or as a way of sounding authoritative. Unless certain words or phrases are required due to regulation or contract, use the shorter, simpler word or phrase.
