Address your user
=================

Our documentation often asks the reader to do something or take 
some action. Use the second person singular whenever you can.

> You can create a new template with the Template Editor.
>
> Find out how your JIRA Service Desk account works.

If you create content for multiple users address the primary user 
as *you* and refer to the other users by their role or titles.

> As an Administrator, you can adjust the settings for Team Members in your Admin panel.
